## Patent Reference

- Title: Multi-source heterogeneous human genetic variation data management and annotation method and device for ACMG
- URL: https://patents.google.com/patent/CN117334258A/en

### Abstract

Abstract Translated from Chinese 本申请公开了一种面向ACMG的多源异构人类基因变异数据管理与注释方法及装置。该方法包括：对ACMG致病变异评级相关的多源异构数据的存储方式以及格式进行统一转换形成数据库；在数据库中添加配置项表，将数据源的每个字段在页面展示的信息进行配置且存入该配置项表的对应的列中，在前端页面显示时，调取该配置项表，进行动态显示；根据ACMG指南将注释信息分为变异水平和基因水平两个类型，将配置后的数据库中的数据按照上述两个类型进行归类整合，并将整合后的每列数据通过编程控制显示；通过创建配置表，根据注释需求，为疾病基因变异数据集添加数据源。 This application discloses a multi-source heterogeneous human gene variation data management and annotation method and device for ACMG. The method includes: uniformly converting the storage methods and formats of multi-source heterogeneous data related to ACMG pathogenic variant ratings to form a database; adding a configuration item table in the database, and configuring the information displayed on the page for each field of the data source And stored in the corresponding column of the configuration item table, when displayed on the front-end page, the configuration item table is called for dynamic display; according to the ACMG guidelines, the annotation information is divided into two types: mutation level and gene level, and the configured The data in the database are classified and integrated according to the above two types, and each column of the integrated data is displayed through programming control; by creating a configuration table and adding data sources to the disease gene variation data set according to annotation requirements.

With the development of technological diversity and With the exponential increase in sequencing data, research on interpreting the pathogenicity of genetic variants is facing challenges. The American College of Medical Genetics and Genomics (ACMG) established a working group in 2013 to develop guidelines and standards for the interpretation of sequence variants. Members of the working group include representatives from the ACMG and the Association for Molecular Pathology. Molecular Pathology (AMP) and the College of American Pathologists (CAP). 目前，ACMG基因变异致病性解读标准和指南被广泛认为是识别致病变异的最佳流程，然而ACMG推荐的大多数注释数据资源和变异致病性预测得分遍布整个互联网获取对应的数据十分繁琐，而且部分注释数据必须使用命令行工具才能获取，这使得大部分没有受过生物信息技能训练的临床医生和遗传咨询师难以高效地解读变异的功能和致病性。目前，由于注释数据获取困难，大部分临床医生和遗传咨询师变异的解读缺乏全面且针对性的处理和注释。Currently, ACMG genetic variant pathogenicity interpretation standards and guidelines are widely considered to be the best process for identifying pathogenic variants. However, most of the annotation data resources and variant pathogenicity prediction scores recommended by ACMG are spread across the entire Internet. Obtaining the corresponding data is very cumbersome. , and some annotation data must be obtained using command line tools, which makes it difficult for most clinicians and genetic counselors who are not trained in bioinformatics skills to efficiently interpret the function and pathogenicity of variants.
