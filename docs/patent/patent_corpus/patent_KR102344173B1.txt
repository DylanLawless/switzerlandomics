## Patent Reference

- Title: Coronary Disease Prediction methods using clinical and genetic Risk score in Korean and method for suggesting diagnosis related to Coronary Disease
- URL: https://patents.google.com/patent/KR102344173B1/en

### Abstract

Abstract The present invention relates to a method for predicting the probability of developing the coronary artery disease by integrating clinical information and genetic mutation information of Koreans. More specifically, provided is a method for recommending related tests, which predicts the probability of developing the coronary artery disease at a specific time in Koreans using a disease prediction model built by integrating SNP marker mutation information and clinical information. According to the present invention, the method for predicting the probability of developing the coronary artery disease predicts not only Korean-specific genetic information, but also the probability of developing the coronary artery disease by integrating clinical information for each individual, so that the accuracy is far superior to that of the existing method, so as to be useful for early diagnosis and prevention of the coronary artery disease.
